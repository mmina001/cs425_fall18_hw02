Short Bio

John Doe was born in Florida and now divides his time all over of American. Having worked professionally for many years in poverty-alleviation programmes around the world, he turns to photography to remind himself of the beauty that exists in a world of economic, social and political injustice. His photography focuses on discovering abstract expressions of this beauty in the natural world: in small patches of ice, on the water surfaces, among leaves in tidal pools. John's work has been exhibited in galleries across the United States and has won a number of awards. His images can be found in corporate and private collections in the United States and Europe.
